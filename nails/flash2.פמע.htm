Flash Platinum Gel Polish

1 Review
| Ask a question
$22.50

Flash Platinum is a must-have ultra reflective glitter polish!

Contain 100% super reflective silver fine glitter in clear base, hands down the coolest polish ever! The reflective effects can be captured with the phone flash. It's looks like mini diamonds all over!

Application/ how to apply:

Apply one thin coat over a base colour of your choice or apply on it's own in 3-4 coats for full a full disco ball effects! Use your phone flash for the reflective effects. Best shown over darker colors. 

*All gel polishes are required to cure under LED/UV nail lamp, you can get a holo LED/UV Nail Lamp here.
*After curing, apply a non-wipe gel top coat or remove the tacky layer using isopropyl alcohol.

Product details:

  • 100% ultra reflective glitters 
  • High quality ingredients
  • Scattered sparkles finish
  • Reflective effects
  • Gel formula
  • Safe, cruelty-free and vegan
  • Volume: 12ml (0.4 fl. oz.)

Pictures shown here are Flash Platinum layer over The Blackest Black and on it's own. All swatches are seal with the best selling non-wipe glossy top coat Always On Top! from the Premium Nail Treatment Collection.

Note: The reflective effects can only be seen/captured through phone camera and in real life at certain angles and certain lightings only.

    Get this ultra reflective topper and create a bold, beautiful look that turns heads with every smile by clicking ‘Add to Cart’ above to get yours.

    All our nail polishes are 5-free. They do not contain harmful ingredients such as Dibutyl Phthalate (DBP), Toluene, Formaldehyde, Formaldehyde Resin and Camphor.
